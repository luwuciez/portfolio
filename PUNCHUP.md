## Proposal
For the Playground page, I'd like to create an interactive card carousel for my hobby artworks. The viewer can scroll through the cards, click to enlarge them, and there is also a button that spins the carousel to give you a random card.
I will be using React hooks to implement the scrolling and randomization logic, and GSAP to create the animations.
